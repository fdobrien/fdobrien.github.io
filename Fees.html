Professional Fees and Expenses

(Effective September 1, 2020)

  • Per Diem: $1500.00 Docketing: none
  • Grievance Arbitration: The fee is $1500.00 per day, for hearing, and for research and preparation of the opinion and award. A hearing day is any portion of a day up to eight hours. Time for research and preparation is prorated.
  • Cancellation Policy: If the scheduled hearing is cancelled with notice of less than two calendar weeks (14 days), the per diem fee for each day of hearing shall be charged if another matter cannot be set in its place.
  • Interest Arbitration and Fact-finding: The fee is $1500.00 per day, for up to eight hours. Time for research and preparation is prorated.
  • Labor Mediation: The per diem fee is $1500 per day for any portion of a day, up to eight hours, after which an hourly fee of $200 will be charged for any portion of each hour after the initial eight-hour period.
  • Travel Time: The per diem fee of $1500 for any portion of a travel day in excess of one hour, up to eight hours, with time over eight hours for one day prorated. Travel expenses calculated from the closer of the two business addresses to the parties' site, unless otherwise agreed.
  • Expenses: Actual cost of reasonable expenses including airfare, car rental, food, and lodging. Copying and overnight document shipping charges are billed. Automobile mileage is charged at the current IRS expense rate.
